My mantras

Many of my friends would tell you that I am always motivated to complete all of my duties. However, the fact is that it is more about discipline than motivation. I wouldn't do practically anything in my life if I simply worked because I'm motivated. This is easy to say; its implementation is much more difficult. Undoubtedly.

In my situation, if I feel down or don't feel like doing anything (which is nearly always), I tell myself various statements to help me overcome my laziness and go back to work. They're what I call my mantras. They are as follows:

  1. All I have is all I need.
  2. Someone busier than you is working harder than you.
  3. If it was easy everybody would do it.
  4. There's a lot of people wanting what you want. Work harder
  5. Regardless of how you feel hein ?
  6. The harder the battle, the sweater the victory
  7. Progress is progress no matter how small.
  8. The only way to do it is to do it.

I hope they were motivating and you will pick out some of them to keep you going too. If you didn't notice, most of them aren't from me but they get me inspired. If you found them interesting Contact me and tell me your favorite ones. Go back to work king.
